## 3. Features Section

**Section Title:**
## All The Tools You Need to Succeed

**(This section would be laid out with icons for each feature)**

**Feature 1: Developer Profiles**
Go beyond a resume. Create a rich profile that highlights your technical skills, hackathon roles (Frontend, Backend, UI/UX, Pitching), and collaboration style. Let the community know what you bring to the table.

**Feature 2: The Hackathon Hub**
A comprehensive, searchable directory of global hackathons. Filter by technology (AI, Web3, Mobile), theme, prize money, and more. Never miss an opportunity to build.

**Feature 3: Smart Team Formation**
Our platform makes finding teammates easy. Search for developers by skill, post open roles on your team’s recruiting board, and connect with people who share your vision and passion.

**Feature 4: Project Showcases**
Be proud of what you build. Every project gets a dedicated page with a gallery, description, and link to the code. Get feedback, find inspiration, and build your portfolio.
